## Team roles

Each workspace member has a role that determines what they can do:

| | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Create and edit posts | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Use signatures and labels | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View the calendar and post list | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Manage social accounts | Yes | Yes | No |
| Create and manage labels | Yes | Yes | No |
| Create and manage signatures | Yes | Yes | No |
| Invite and remove members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Change member roles | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage workspace settings | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage API keys | Yes | Yes | No |
| Manage billing | Yes | No | No |
| Delete workspace | Yes | No | No |

Each workspace has exactly **one owner** — the user who created it. The owner role cannot be transferred.

## Inviting members

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the email address
4. Select a role (Admin or Member)
5. Click **Send Invite**

The invited person receives an email with a link to join. If they don't have a TryPost account, they'll be prompted to create one first.

<Note>
  Pending invitations can be cancelled at any time before they're accepted.
</Note>

## Accepting an invite

When you receive an invitation:

1. Click the link in the email
2. Log in or create a TryPost account
3. Accept or decline the invitation
4. If accepted, you're immediately added to the workspace

## Changing roles

Owners and admins can change a member's role at any time from **Settings > Team**. Click on the member's current role to change it.

## Removing members

Owners and admins can remove members from **Settings > Team**. Removing a member:

- Revokes their access immediately
- Does **not** delete posts they created
- Does **not** affect their TryPost account or other workspace memberships

The removed member receives a notification about the removal.

## Multiple workspaces

A single TryPost account can belong to multiple workspaces with different roles in each. For example:

- **Owner** of your personal workspace
- **Admin** of your company workspace
- **Member** of a client workspace

Use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to navigate between them.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple owners?">
    No. Each workspace has exactly one owner — the person who created it. Use the Admin role for other team members who need full management access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a member see all posts or only their own?">
    All members can see all posts in the workspace, regardless of who created them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I remove someone who has scheduled posts?">
    Their scheduled posts remain and will still be published as planned. Only access to the workspace is revoked.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on team members?">
    On TryPost Cloud, yes — your plan sets the cap (Starter 1, Plus 5, Pro 15, Max 20). When you hit it, sending a new invite returns `402 Payment Required`. Self-hosted instances are unlimited.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
